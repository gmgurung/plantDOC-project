Our AI Model

At the heart of PlantDoc lies our advanced AI model, designed to diagnose plant diseases with unprecedented accuracy.

Model Architecture

Our model is built using TensorFlow and Keras, incorporating state-of-the-art neural network architectures for image recognition. It processes images with a batch size of 32 and an input size of 256x256 pixels across 15 channels.

The model has been trained for 50 epochs on the PlantVillage dataset, which includes over 2,500 images spanning 13 distinct plant species. This extensive training enables our model to accurately identify a wide range of plant diseases.

Performance and Accuracy

The effectiveness of our model is evident in its precision and the confidence level it achieves in predictions. Through rigorous testing and optimization, we ensure that our model meets the high standards required for real-world applications in agriculture.
